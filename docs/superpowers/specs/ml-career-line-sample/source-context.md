# ML-career "The Line" — source context + authoring log (experience-composer, PERSONAL-PAGE surface)

The PERSONAL-PAGE entry in the five-surface quality test of the
experience-composer pipeline. Surface was **given** (personal-page); the fill is
content-only against `TheLineFill` (world-template `the-line` /
`home-career-project-timeline`), handed to the shipped `TheLineTemplate`.

Because the personal-page honesty rule forbids inventing biography for a real
person, the subject is an **explicitly-marked illustrative composite persona** —
Nadia Osei, an applied-ML engineer. Her ~14-year career is the synthetic part;
its SPINE is the field's real, dated public epochs. `chrome.syntheticMark` (and
the footer + accessible hero notice) declare exactly this.

- **Synthetic persona declared:** Nadia Osei, her employers ("a retail bank", a

  Declared in `chrome.syntheticMark` ("ILLUSTRATIVE PERSONA · REAL EPOCHS"),

- **Real epochs listed:** the six field milestones in the table above are real,
  dated, and sourced; each station answers the milestone of its year. This is the
  one part of the page that is NOT synthetic, and the notices say so ("only the
  field milestones the line answers to are real and dated").
- **The switchback (the honest anomaly):** the 2023–2024 management detour —
  took an engineering-manager role, stepped back down to an IC seat a year later
  when the agentic wave broke without her. A genuine, unglamorous career reversal
  left in rather than airbrushed; structurally flagged as the one `switchback`
  node and mirrored in the register table.
- **No real-person facts invented:** per the personal-page rule, no real
  individual is named or described; the composite exists precisely so no
  biography is fabricated about anyone real.
