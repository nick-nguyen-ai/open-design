## Fit note (honest)

Better than it first looks, but with one structural strain. The deck's register — "an
honest argument that keeps its worst week in view" — is a near-perfect voice for the
GUIDANCE honesty doctrine, and its hard-wired anomaly conceit (WEEK 37 REGIME CHANGE —
FLAGGED, NOT SMOOTHED) maps exactly onto the one-real-anomaly rule. The strain: the whole
template threads ONE numeric time-series through every slide, and the intro content has no
measured series — the fill would lean on a fully synthetic growth curve, which the honesty
rules permit (notice-covered, magnitude-anchored) but which makes the specimen's most
prominent visual element invented. And it is all moot today: the template contract does
not exist. Recommend templatizing The Long Signal first (separate task), or shooting
signal-glass's dashboard exemplar `db-model-monitoring-cockpit` (a published template) as
the specimen with a content-held-constant caveat.
